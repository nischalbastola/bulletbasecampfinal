Bobby

Bobby is the go-to guy for off-road adventures and local secrets. With a knack for making every ride fun and safe, he’s the team’s energy booster and a favorite among riders. Fluent in three languages and always ready with a joke or a helping hand, Bobby makes every tour unforgettable.

  • Expert in off-road and adventure routes
  • Fluent in English, Nepali, and Hindi
  • Known for his humor and positive energy
  • Passionate about local culture and history
  • Always ready to help riders in tough spots
“Every detour is a new story waiting to happen.”
